Privacy Policy — LNR - Work Time

Last updated: July 2026

Introduction

LNR - Work Time is a work-time tracking app. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data (if any) is collected when you use the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

LNR - Work Time does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. Specifically, it does not collect:

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or fitness data
  • Usage analytics, tracking, or advertising identifiers

Data Stored on Your Device

Any work-time entries you create are stored locally on your device only. This data is never sent to any server and is not accessible to us or to any third party. It is erased if you uninstall the app or clear the app's storage.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing services.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
